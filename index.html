About the Blog

Welcome to Great British Bike Off, where cycling meets culinary adventure! I'm passionate about exploring Britain's beautiful countryside on two wheels, discovering hidden gems, charming villages, and the best food stops along the way.

From challenging hill climbs in the Peak District to leisurely rides through the Cotswolds, each journey is an opportunity to experience the rich tapestry of British culture, landscape, and cuisine. Join me as I share routes, restaurant recommendations, and the stories that make each ride memorable.
